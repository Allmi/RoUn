Lysara Map Navigator

Lysara is not arranged by streets. It is arranged by who lives close enough to power to pretend it was an accident.

Hover or tap the pins. This version adds the residential layer: who lives near each district, who works there, and why the map matters even when no active commission has been generated yet. This is a permanent city guide, not a case log for one specific playthrough.

Route shadows: highlight where each fixed route pressure most often leaves traces across the city.
